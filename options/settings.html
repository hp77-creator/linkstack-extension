Keyboard Shortcuts

You can set up a keyboard shortcut to quickly open LinkStack:

  1. Visit chrome://extensions/shortcuts in your browser
  2. Find "LinkStack" in the list
  3. Click the edit icon (pencil) next to "Open LinkStack sync menu"
  4. Press your desired key combination (e.g., Ctrl+Shift+L or Command+Shift+L)
Choose a shortcut that's easy to remember and doesn't conflict with other browser shortcuts
